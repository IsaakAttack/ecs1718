Mahatma Gandhi led the people of India to independence while they were under British control.
Gandhi also helped the peasents and poor farmers out of extreme taxes and discrimination.
He also inspired peaceful protests around the world, including Martin Luther King Jr's peaceful protests against racism in the 1960s.
