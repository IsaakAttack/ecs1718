Porgs

A Porg is a fictional animal from Star Wars: The Last Jedi, where it first appears in the Star Wars franchise.

Porgs are mainly found on the planet Ahch-To. They can only fly for a short amount of time, so they never leave their islands. Porgs mainly feed on fish they catch out of the ocean by diving.
